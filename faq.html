Frequently Asked Questions

What is Seenn?

Seenn is an AI visibility tracking platform that helps e-commerce brands monitor and improve their presence across AI search engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini. We show you exactly how AI talks about your products and help you outrank competitors.

How does it work?

Simply upload your products (manually or via URL), and we'll generate hundreds of queries that customers typically ask AI about products like yours. We then track your visibility across multiple AI platforms and provide insights on how to improve your rankings.

Which AI platforms do you track?

We currently track ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, and DeepSeek. We're constantly adding more platforms as they become relevant for e-commerce.

How accurate is the tracking?

We run multiple queries throughout the day to ensure accuracy. AI responses can vary, so we aggregate results to give you the most reliable visibility score possible.

Can I track my competitors?

Yes! Our competitor analysis feature shows you how your competitors rank for the same queries and provides suggestions on how to outrank them.

How do I get started?

Click "Get Started" or "Start Free Trial" on our homepage. You'll be able to upload your first products and see initial results within minutes.
