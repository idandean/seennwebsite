We Know the Pain of Chasing Payments — Because We Lived It

Seenn was born from a real problem in a real family business. Not in a lab. Not from a pitch deck. From the daily grind of trying to get paid.

Our Story

We ran a family business in Israel. Like most small business owners, we were good at what we did — delivering value, serving clients, building relationships. But there was one part of the job that nobody prepared us for: getting paid on time. Every month, the same painful cycle would begin. Invoices would go out. Due dates would pass. And then the uncomfortable work would start — the follow-up calls, the awkward emails, the mental gymnastics of trying to collect what we were owed without damaging the relationships we had worked so hard to build.

It took a real toll. Hours that should have been spent growing the business were instead spent chasing overdue payments. Cash flow suffered. Stress levels climbed. And the worst part was knowing that every other small and medium business owner we talked to was dealing with the exact same thing. This was not our problem — it was a universal one. Millions of businesses worldwide lose time, money, and energy on manual payment collection every single day.

That realization is what led us to build Seenn. We asked a simple question: what if an AI agent could handle the entire collections process — professionally, persistently, and without ever making things awkward with your clients? What if business owners could stop dreading the end of the month and start focusing on what they actually love doing? So we built it. Not as technologists looking for a problem to solve, but as business operators who had lived the problem and refused to accept it any longer.

Our Mission

We believe that business owners should spend their time building, creating, and growing — not buried in back-office operations that drain their energy. Our mission is to deploy AI agents that handle the repetitive, time-consuming work that holds businesses back. We started with payment collection because we knew the pain firsthand. But our vision is bigger: a world where every small and medium business has access to the same operational efficiency that only large enterprises could afford before. Based in Israel and serving businesses across the US and Israel, we are building the future of automated business operations — one AI agent at a time.
